A Bangladeshi band founded in 2016 by Aether Hasan and Ishmam Intiser. It all started with their deep fascination for blues, rock and roll, progressive rock, and psychedelic rock.

Our first release, “Brihospoti,” remains a cherished milestone, marking the start of everything. Over the years, we have been working on our “kalponik” debut album – Train Poka which shall be ready this year alongside another album – Jacchi Kothay.
